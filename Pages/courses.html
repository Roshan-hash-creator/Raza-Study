Target for JEE & NEET

At Raza Study, we understand that cracking NEET and JEE requires unwavering dedication and a structured approach to learning. Our TARGET program is meticulously designed to cater to the specific needs of aspiring medical and engineering professionals, providing them with the tools and support they need to excel in these highly competitive entrance exams.

Pre - Medical & JEE ( 11th & 12th )

At Raza Study, Our Pre-Medical & JEE (11th & 12th) program is designed to provide students with a thorough understanding of the core concepts and principles that form the foundation of medical and engineering education. We emphasize conceptual clarity and problem-solving skills to ensure that students are well-prepared for the challenges ahead.

Pre - Nurture ( 9th & 10th )

At Raza Study, we understand that the foundation of academic excellence is laid in the early years of schooling. Our Pre-Nurture program, designed specifically for students in classes 9th and 10th, is aimed at nurturing young minds and preparing them for the rigors of higher education.

Our Pre-Nurture program goes beyond rote memorization and emphasizes the development of a deep understanding of concepts. We encourage our students to ask questions, explore different perspectives, and develop a genuine love for learning.

At Raza Study, Our Crash Course program provides comprehensive coverage of all the essential topics and concepts required for success in PAT, PET, PPHT, and Nursing entrance exams. Our experienced and dedicated faculty is committed to providing each student with personalized attention. We understand that every student learns differently, and we tailor our teaching approach to suit your individual needs.
